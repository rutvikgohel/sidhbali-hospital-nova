Excellent Medical Services

Emergency Care

At Sidhbali Hospital, we understand that medical emergencies can happen at any time. Our Emergency Care Unit is open 24/7, providing rapid, life-saving treatment when you need it most. Our highly skilled doctors, nurses, and emergency responders are trained to handle urgent medical conditions with expertise, speed, and compassion.

Operation & Surgery

At Sidhbali Hospital, we provide safe, effective, and advanced surgical care for patients of all ages. Our team of highly skilled surgeons, anesthesiologists, and surgical nurses use the latest medical technology to ensure the best outcomes for every procedure.

Outdoor Checkup

At Sidhbali Hospital, we believe that healthcare should be accessible, convenient, and stress-free. Our Outdoor Checkup Services allow patients to receive medical consultations and basic healthcare services outside traditional hospital settings, whether in outdoor clinics, community health camps, or at-home visits.

Ambulance Service

At Sidhbali Hospital, we provide 24/7 ambulance services to ensure quick and efficient medical transport for patients in need of urgent care.

Medicine & Pharmacy

At Sidhbali Hospital, our Medicine & Pharmacy Services provide safe, reliable, and high-quality medications to support your health and recovery

Blood Testing

At Sidhbali Hospital, our Blood Testing Services provide fast, accurate, and comprehensive lab tests to help diagnose and monitor various health conditions.
